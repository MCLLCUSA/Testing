Our Mission

FLYMOMS provides scholarships and resources to inspire the next generation of aviators. We empower teen students with the training, mentorship, and opportunities they need to pursue careers and hobbies in aviation — opening the skies to those who dream of flight.

🎓 Scholarships

Funding flight training, ground school, and certification costs for students in need.

👨‍🏫 Mentorship

Connecting aspiring aviators with experienced pilots for guidance and inspiration.

✈️ Flight Opportunities

Creating pathways to airfield experiences, discovery flights, and career exposure.

Every donation brings a young dreamer closer to the cockpit.
